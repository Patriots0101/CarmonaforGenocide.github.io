Does Carmona have to accept all donations?

No. Not at all.

The NYS Campaign Finance Board allows refunds of contributions and has stated such in their website (NYS BOE).

In 2022, Jonathan Soto, a state assembly candidate at the time, “returned a $4,700 donation from electronic trading exec Michael Jenkins” (The City).

Carmona can do the same, but has failed to, and shows no intention in refunding the contributions highlighted above.

Why does it matter?

The source of campaign funding can influence policies and decisions made by elected officials. By understanding where Johanna Carmona's support comes from, voters can make informed decisions about her candidacy and her commitment to representing their interests.
